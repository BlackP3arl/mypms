**Pro Tips for Posting:**

1. **Best Times to Post:**
   - Weekdays: 8-10am, 12-1pm, 5-6pm (local time)
   - Avoid late nights and weekends for announcements

2. **Visual Assets:**
   - Always include screenshots or GIFs when possible
   - Show the product in action (drag-and-drop)
   - Use high-quality, colorful images

3. **Engagement Strategy:**
   - Reply to every comment in first hour
   - Ask questions to encourage responses
   - RT user testimonials and success stories

4. **A/B Testing:**
   - Test different CTAs (Try it, Get started, Check it out)
   - Test with/without emojis
   - Test question vs. statement tweets

5. **Content Mix (80/20 Rule):**
   - 80% value (tips, insights, education)
   - 20% promotion (try MyPMS, sign up)
