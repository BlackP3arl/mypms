## Voice & Tone Guidelines

### Brand Voice
- **Friendly**: Conversational, approachable, human
- **Confident**: We know our product is great
- **Transparent**: Honest about features and limitations
- **Helpful**: Focus on solving user problems
- **Modern**: Tech-savvy without being alienating

### Writing Style
- Use active voice
- Keep sentences short and punchy
- Break up text with whitespace
- Use "you" and "your" (user-focused)
- Avoid jargon and buzzwords
- Be specific, not vague

### Examples

‚ùå Don't say: "MyPMS leverages cutting-edge technologies to facilitate seamless task orchestration."

‚úÖ Do say: "MyPMS helps you organize tasks with drag-and-drop simplicity."

‚ùå Don't say: "Our solution provides enterprise-grade functionality."

‚úÖ Do say: "Simple enough for individuals. Powerful enough for teams."

‚ùå Don't say: "Utilize MyPMS to enhance your productivity paradigm."

‚úÖ Do say: "Use MyPMS to get more done."
